# 🔌 Circuit Connections

## 🧠 Arduino Uno Connections

### 📡 Ultrasonic Sensor (HC-SR04)

* VCC → 5V
* GND → GND
* TRIG → Digital Pin 9
* ECHO → Digital Pin 10

---

### 🔄 Servo Motor (SG90)

* VCC (Red) → 5V
* GND (Brown/Black) → GND
* Signal (Orange/Yellow) → Digital Pin 11

---

## ⚠️ Important Notes

* Ensure common ground between all components
* Do not power servo from external source unless needed
* Keep connections tight to avoid signal noise
* Use breadboard for stable connections

---

## 🔋 Power Supply

* Arduino powered via USB (5V)
* All components draw power from Arduino

---

## 📌 Pin Summary

| Component    | Pin Connection |

| HC-SR04 TRIG | D9             |
| HC-SR04 ECHO | D10            |
| Servo Signal | D11            |
